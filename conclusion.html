Conclusion

Coherence warns us to appreciate and make the best of what we have. Choices determine everything, big and small. The mysterious phone call ending makes us wonder if the universe will allow you to change your destiny. Does that Emily manage to force herself into a more appealing reality where she doesn’t belong, or will our choices always haunt us?

Nothing is random.
